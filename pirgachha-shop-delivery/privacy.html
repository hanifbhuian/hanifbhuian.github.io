Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Pirgachha Shop & Delivery collects customer name, phone number, delivery address, order details, payment method, and optional location link to process local delivery orders.

How we use information

We use customer information only to confirm orders, collect items from shops/restaurants/pharmacies, deliver orders, contact customers, and keep service records.

Location information

If a customer clicks “Add My Location,” the browser may request permission to access device location. The location is converted into a Google Maps link and included in the order message only when the customer chooses to share/send the order.

Sharing

Order details may be shared with assigned delivery riders and partner shops only for completing delivery. We do not sell customer information.

Medicine orders

Basic medicine delivery is a delivery support service only. Prescription or restricted medicine should be handled by licensed pharmacies and may require a valid prescription.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Pirgachha Shop & Delivery using the phone number or communication method shown on the website.
